Introduction

This hospital was built as a countermeasure to treat the ever increasing patients, who are suffering from COVID. It is a temporary facility to better serve citizens at this hard times.

We are trying to solve this issue by creating a website where people can register themselves, fill out a self-assessment form to check if they are suffering from COVID, instead of going directly to the hospital. This decreases the crowds at the hospitals. Now, the Nurses in hospitals analyze the patient assessment forms and decide if they require any medical attention or not.

Mission

The number of cases in Montreal is increasing exponentially day-by-day. Since most of the symptoms are similar to Flu, people are rushing to the hospital without proper guidance resulting in unnecessary crowding thereby wasting valuable resources at the hospitals. Our aim is to:

  • Increase the efficiency of hospital and decrease the usage of valuable resources.
  • Increase the awarness among people and decrease the panic.
  • Providing appointments with Nurses and Doctors, thereby decreasing the crowd.
  • Providing Self-assesment tools and COVID testing
